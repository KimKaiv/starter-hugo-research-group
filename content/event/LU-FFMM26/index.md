<br> <br>
CRUCIAL has taken the initiative to sponsor a special session on prediction markets in the [2026 Financial Fraud, Misconduct and Market Manipulation Conference](https://wp.lancs.ac.uk/ffmm2026/) to be held at [Lancaster University Management School (LUMS)](https://www.lancaster.ac.uk/lums/) 10--11 September. 
<br> <br>
One of the papers in this session will be selected for the CRUCIAL Award for the Best Prediction Markets Paper.
